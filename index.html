Welcome To Sizemore Services

"I'm secure, & bondable, with an understanding on how to take care of a home. I pride myself on being friendly, with a reliable service at a competitive price." –Raven Sizemore.

After being in business for over 20 years, Sizemore Services knows how to get the job done right. Raven Sizemore, the owner/operator, started doing part time labor work for a property management company. After the company went under, he started doing this full time on his own, because he loves what he does.

He has successfully completed jobs for large, commercial properties and for residential properties. No job is too big or too small! Sizemore even has experience in electrical, plumbing, roofing, & construction.
